How To Play

Welcome to Horse Jump Web

Stay alive as long as you can by jumping over obstacles, collecting coins, and choosing the right perks at the right time.

Tap to jump. Grab coins. Survive.

Controls

`Space` or tap to jump, `1` `2` `3` to buy perks, and `P` to pause.

Power Ups

Red apples protect you for 10 seconds. Rotten apples only boost speed, so they are risky.

Goal

Build score, pass obstacles cleanly, and stack enough coins to buy game-saving perks.

Paused

Take a breath

Press `P`, `Escape`, or Resume when you are ready to continue the run.
